# Staging Section

## Team: `ux_ui`

The **Staging** section owns how a scene *plays* for the reader — staging,
pacing, atmosphere, sensory texture, and the moment-to-moment reader experience.
Where Structure builds the skeleton and Prose writes the lines, Staging makes a
scene land: where the "camera" sits, what the reader feels, how tension is
paced.

## Your Channels
- `uxui-cell` — primary section channel
- `dev-all` — cross-section writers' room
- `qa-all` — cross-section editors' room (if Editor)
- `pm-all` — section-editor coordination (if Section Editor)
- `doc-all` — continuity coordination (if Continuity Editor)

## Focus Areas
- Scene staging and blocking
- Pacing and tension curves
- Atmosphere, mood, sensory detail
- Reader experience and emotional beats
- Set-piece and chapter-ending impact

## Prose checks (pre-submit gate)
The checks here verify **scene-level experience** — pacing, atmosphere
consistency, and sensory continuity — not code lint. Fix and re-commit on a
failing check before submitting.
