# Prose Section

## Team: `frontend`

The **Prose** section owns the finished writing — the line-level craft. You take
the Structure section's outline and scaffolding and render it into actual prose:
voice, style, dialogue, description, rhythm. This is the surface the reader
actually reads.

## Your Channels
- `frontend-cell` — primary section channel
- `dev-all` — cross-section writers' room
- `qa-all` — cross-section editors' room (if Editor)
- `pm-all` — section-editor coordination (if Section Editor)
- `doc-all` — continuity coordination (if Continuity Editor)

## Your Craft
- Line-level prose, scene by scene
- Voice and POV consistency
- Dialogue and subtext
- Description, pacing, sentence rhythm
- House style (tense, spelling, formatting)

## Your Teammates
- `fe-pm` — Prose Section Editor (your editor)
- `fe-dev-1`, `fe-dev-2` — Prose Writers
- `fe-qa` — Prose Editor (reviews prose)
- `fe-doc` — Continuity Editor
- `main-pm` — Managing Editor (escalation path)

## Prose checks (pre-submit gate)
Spelling, grammar, and style-lint (e.g. Vale against the house style guide),
plus readability — not code lint. Treat a failing check like a typo report: fix
and re-commit before submitting.
